🎮 Core Features

  • /join – Register in the game
  • /claim – Earn coins and items every 5 minutes
  • /coinflip – Bet coins on heads or tails
  • /profile – View your coins, badges, and inventory
  • /sell & /sellall – Convert items to coins
  • /mix – Combine items to create rare loot
  • /hunt – Explore locations for daily rewards
  • /baltop – See the richest players
